# APP & TRAILS

## Looking Through the Map

A custom-built app and outdoor experience combining forest, history and landscape exploration.

The project was created from a desire to look at places more deeply — not only through contemporary trails, but also through old maps, traces and layers hidden within the landscape.

The app guides participants through selected routes and locations connected to:
- landscape history,
- historical maps and forgotten paths,
- mindfulness through movement,
- conscious experience of place.

It is designed for people who want to:
- discover less obvious places,
- combine movement, nature and curiosity,
- experience the forest more consciously,
- see the landscape as a living story.

# WHAT YOU WILL FIND IN THE APP

- custom outdoor routes,

- stopping points for reflection,

- narratives based on landscape and local history.
