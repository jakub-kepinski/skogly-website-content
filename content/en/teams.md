# FOR TEAMS

I work with teams in environments designed to restore attention, improve communication and create space for meaningful dialogue.

I combine facilitation, outdoor experiences and the OM method — a custom framework built around three steps:

Reveal — see the situation more clearly  
Reframe — shift the perspective for action  
Root — strengthen what already works

These are not “team-building attractions”.

They are carefully designed experiences created to:
- improve collaboration,
- build trust,
- clarify communication,
- help teams move forward together.

For more than 18 years, I have worked with organizations, leaders and teams — both in Poland and internationally.
