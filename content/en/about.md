# ABOUT SKOGLY

## Introduction

Inspired by Scandinavia, created in Poland.

The word “skog” means forest in both Swedish and Norwegian. The two runes forming the logo — Sowilo and Kaunan — are also the first letters of the name.

Sowilo symbolizes the sun, clarity and strength. Kaunan, often translated as “torch”, relates to knowledge and the search for it. Combined together as a bindrune, they stand as a counterbalance to the well-known Bluetooth rune — a symbol of technology and the digital world.

# ABOUT ME

For nearly two decades, I have been connected to the world of education — both as a teacher in schools in the UK and Poland, and as an internal trainer and facilitator in corporate environments.

Alongside that, I step outside the walls as a certified cycling tourism guide and forest bathing guide.

---

# PHILOSOPHY

Skogly is a way to breathe out from screens.

Depending on the need, this can take a more active form through movement, or move towards forest therapy, mindfulness and slowing down.

I see the forest as a space for restoring attention, regulation and reconnecting with what feels natural — both within ourselves and around us.

# PLACE

I invite you to Oborniki Śląskie — the green lungs of Wrocław, located within the Trzebnica Hills region.

This is where I lead forest bathing sessions, walks and outdoor experiences rooted in adventure, calm and learning through nature.
