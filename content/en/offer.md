# OFFER

## Forest Bathing (Shinrin-yoku)

Guided nature experiences based on mindfulness, sensory awareness and the calm rhythm of the forest.

This is neither trekking nor a regular walk. It is a space for regeneration and restoring attention — guided by a certified forest bathing guide.

Designed for people who want to:
- reduce tension and overload,
- slow down and take a break from screens,
- reconnect with their body and surroundings.

Sessions take place around Oborniki Śląskie and the Barycz Valley — individually or in small groups.

## Looking Through the Map

Outdoor micro-adventures combining nature, history and landscape exploration.

We work with maps, a custom field app and traces hidden in the forest — old roads, forgotten places and stories embedded in the landscape.

Designed for people who want to:
- experience the forest and landscape differently,
- explore places beyond obvious trails,
- combine movement, curiosity and connection with nature.

Each route is an original experience rooted in real terrain and historical layers of the landscape.

---

## Forest & Bike

A combination of calm cycling and regeneration in nature.

The bicycle becomes a tool for exploration — a way to move further, slower and more consciously through the landscape.

During the experience:
- part of the route is covered by bike,
- part takes place in the forest through elements of forest bathing,
- the whole journey becomes one connected experience of movement and slowing down.

Designed for people who:
- want to stay active without pressure,
- seek balance instead of performance,
- enjoy meaningful outdoor experiences with structure and calm.
